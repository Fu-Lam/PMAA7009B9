About Us

Deeply engaged in compliance technology, empowering financial innovation.

Founded in 2026, FinCheck is co-founded by a group of Master students from HKU Business School. Our mission is to lower the barrier to enterprise compliance through technical means, so that innovation is no longer limited by complex legal provisions.


At FinCheck, we believe that compliance should not be a stumbling block to innovation, but rather a moat for the steady development of enterprises.
